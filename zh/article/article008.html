业主可免费上网查询地(屋)契资料

 

源自Business Times 8月31日报道。

新加坡土地管理局推出线上服务MyProperty, 让业主免费查询他们的地(屋)契和有关房产土地界限测量图。过去公众需要支付介于S$5.25-S$16才能获取有关信息。

公众可以用自己的SingPass登录访问www.sla.gov.sg/MyProperty。该项服务涵盖所有的私人住宅和13万间政府组屋单位。其余的组屋单位屋契信息预计在2019年年底就绪。

The Singapore Land Authority (SLA) has launched a new online service, MyProperty, to let property owners view their title deeds and boundary plans Currently, members of the public have to buy the information via SLA's Integrated Land Information Service or over the counter, paying between $5.25 and $16.

With MyProperty, property owners can now view their own title information without charge.

The service is available at www.sla.gov.sg/MyProperty. Property owners can access the service by logging in with their SingPass. It includes all private property titles and about 130,000 HDB property titles digitised so far.
